“Amor Fati” which is a Latin phrase meaning “love of one’s fate”. What it means is to have the attitude in which everything that happens in your life, including suffering and loss, is good or at the very least necessary. I’ve chosen this motto and way of thinking because I’ve applied this worldview in my own life when facing hardship and I believe it is the correct mindset to have for future obstacles and occasions. This ideology along with other principles of stoicism resonate deeply with me.
